About

A 15 year war in Congo killed more than 5 million people and left an estimated 2 million orphans and 1 million widows. Yet it did not make headline news.

Assistance to Less fortunate, Orphans and Widows (ALOW) was formed to change the lives of these vulnerable children and widows - as many as we can. Initially, we have started this organization in Ottawa, Canada. We would like to see this institution as an international organization in near future.
